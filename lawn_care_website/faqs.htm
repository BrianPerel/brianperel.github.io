FAQ's

What towns do you service?

We are located in Framingham, MA, and service the following areas: Natick, Ashland, and Newton.



How old do I have to be to work at Sunshine?

We hire at the age of 18. This is for safety purposes.

Will Sunshine Lawncare just mow my lawn?

Yes we provide just a mowing package. This is our standard package and very popular because many people just need grass cut.


What are your hours?

We are open Monday-Friday 9am-5pm and Saturday 9am-1pm.




What kind of packages do you provide?

We provide many deals that include different services. Some packages include gardening, tree/branch removal, and snow packages. If you are interested in pricing, check out our service page and see what other deals we have to offer.


Is your company eco-friendly?

Yes! Sunshine Lawncare is known for its eco-friendly products. We try to keep green to make our Earth and customers happy.

Do I have to be present while you are servicing my house?

You do not have to be present while we are at your house. We will leave the bill in the door, and you have 21 days to pay.


What seasons are you open?

We are open all season long. Our company does provide snow plowing and summer lawn care packages.

Can I have a service in the spring and fall to treat my lawn after the winter?

Yes Sunshine does provide a package for standard irrigation in the spring and fall. We will treat your lawn properly to keep it green all year.
