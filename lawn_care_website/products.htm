Product Quality And Service

At Sunshine Lawn Care, we only work with the best lawn care products on the market. We here at Lawn Care, believe that the best lawns must use the top products. We have researched and looked through many communities to find out what are the best products out there! take a look at what we use!


Seed and Fertilizer

For seed and fertilizer, we love to use Scotts Turf Builder Lawn Food. For a healthy, thick green lawn, Lawn food thickens your grass to crowd out new weeds and protects it from future problems! We rely on these products heavily, and we know for sure, Lawn Food won't let you down!

Pest Control

To get rid of those pesky critters that you can't see in the ground, we trust Triazicide Insect Killer. It Kills over 100+ lawn destructive critters and lasts up to 3 months. It is also a great pest control when it comes to gardening!

Weed Killer

Everyone hates having to pick out weeds, but you won't have to worry about that again. Roundup Weed Killer is our goto product to stop weeds from growing! Roundup maintains a key enzyme that plants and bacteria use to make proteins. Once Roundup is absorbed into the leaves, it moves quickly through the plants and accumulates in areas of active growth!
