About Us

Our company is a family-owned company for 25 years. Over the years, we have built strong relations with our customers. Our company's goal is to make you happy and love your yard. Our workers are very friendly and are there to get the job done.

Sunshine Lawn is one of the few companies that travels around neighboring towns of Framingham, MA. With a variety of customers, many of them are satisfied with our job well-done. Along with our location benefits, Sunshine Lawn has very affordable prices! As we offer a variety of special packages and standard packages, our customers can custom plan how they want their lawn to look.

Company History

Sunshine Lawn is an award-winning company. As we have been awarded for many lawn designs and for our customer service, many people are attracted to our company. Our workers have always worked their hardest. In recent years, we have expanded our locations from Framingham to neighboring towns such as Natick, Ashland, and Newton. With locations in these towns, we are able to provide for more customers. These towns also have their own locations and walk-in stores where you can see what services we provide or stop in to say hi to staff!
