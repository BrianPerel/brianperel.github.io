Calisthenics


I am interested in Calisthenics, a fun and revolutionary bodyweight strength-training activity. Here you will see my skills relating to this activity. Filled with innovative movements, I can demonstrate tremendous athletic performance. Calisthenics is a hobby of mine that builds and keeps me fit, lean, healthy, and strong. It provides key benefits such as discipline, coordination, cardiovascular-fitness, balance, agility, and strength. Each exercise offers many variations bringing you the potential of maximum muscle toning. This includes handstands, levers, muscle ups, planches, one-arm pull-ups, single-leg squats, and one-arm push-ups. Calisthenics is the combination of using your body and gravity. Additionally, a pull-up bar, parallel bars, resistance bands, chalk/workout gloves and gym rings can allow for a more advanced workout.
